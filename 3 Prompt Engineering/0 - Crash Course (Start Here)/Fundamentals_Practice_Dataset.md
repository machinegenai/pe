## Closing exercise (slide 24)

Weak prompt to improve: **"Summarize these comments for leadership."**

Ask learners to rebuild it with CO-STAR against this dataset. A strong answer will include: who they are and what the material is (Context), an action like *group and count* (Objective), concise/neutral (Style + Tone), the leader who'll read it (Audience), and an exact output shape with safety flagged first (Response) — plus "do not infer facts not stated."

**Facilitator check:** a correct output groups into the five themes with the counts above and puts the gas pump first as a safety flag.
